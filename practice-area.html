HOME / PRACTICE AREAS

personal Injury

Even minor injuries can evolve into more substantial harm in the long run. We want to ensure that you receive the healthcare you need for your injuries now, the immediate future, and long-term if necessary.

Slip and Fall

Under California law, a slip-and-fall injury claim can be filed only within a finite period of time, so it is in your best interest to act quickly. Let the Maineri Law Firm handle your case and seek the justice and compensation you deserve.

Animal Attack

It is not unusual for animal attack victims to require years of medical attention and psychiatric care. Often the psychological harm can be even more destructive than the physical injuries.

Trucking Accidents

Trucking accidents are more common than one would imagine. Accidents such as these have devastating consequences. We are here to guide you through every step of the legal process.

Vehicle vs. Pedestrian

Being struck by a vehicle is devastating and can result in severe injury and financial burden. The Maineri Law Firm knows how to analyze the circumstances of an accident to determine fault, properly place liability, and get the recovery our clients deserve.

Motorcycle Accidents

During motorcycle accidents, victims often hit the pavement or road barriers. These impacts can lead to serious, even life-threatening spinal cord and brain injuries. Allow the Maineri Law Firm to handle your injury claim so that you can focus on your recovery.

Wrongful Death

In the state of California, many people do not know where to turn for legal advice following the unexpected, accidental death of a loved one. Although every wrongful death claim is different, You can rest assured that as a client of the Maineri Law Firm, your case will be handled with utmost care and thoroughness.

Car Accidents

Unfortunately, car accidents happen when we least expect them. Regardless of the severity of the accident, whether it’s merely a ‘fender bender’ or serious accident resulting in fatality, we urge you to hire an attorney.

Bicycle Accidents

No matter how careful you are, bicycle-related accidents can still happen. At the Maineri Law Firm, we question every eyewitness and investigate each and every detail. We understand that both the witnesses’ recollections, as well as the on-site evidence, can fade or become useless very quickly following any roadside accident or any traffic collision.

Bus Accidents

If someone is injured due to a bus-related accident, they are entitled to get reimbursed completely for all their injury-related and medical treatment expenses under California law. At the Maineri Law Firm, we will study the details of your injury and accident thoroughly and discuss your legal options.
